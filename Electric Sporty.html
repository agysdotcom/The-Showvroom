The Range Rover Sport SV Carbon: Today’s Special Edition Range Rover Sport

RR rolls out latest in a long line of big fat specials. The latest special edition Range Rover Sport follows a tradition of exclusive models, and this time, it's the bronzed, sporty and lightweight Range Rover Sport SV Carbon.

Forged Carbon: Exterior and Options

It’s not a surprise: as its name hints, the SV Carbon is packed with carbon fiber, specifically through a standard forged carbon exterior pack. Expect carbon details on the bonnet, exhaust surrounds, and more. Customers can select from four paint colors or dive into curated hues from the SV premium palette.

Interestingly, despite the model’s name, the signature 23in carbon wheels are optional. Standard fitment is a set of forged alloy wheels with black anodized calipers, with carbon ceramic brakes also available as upgrades.

Interior Luxury & Options

The theme continues inside: you’ll find carbon detailing on seatbacks and dash, plus a range of color and upholstery choices. Prefer a vegan cabin? The SV Carbon offers Ultrafabrics—a premium, leather-free finish.

Power and Performance

Every SV Carbon packs a thundering 4.4-litre V8 hybrid, delivering 626bhp and 590lb ft of torque. It’s good for 180mph where allowed, and sheds 76kg compared to the standard SV, thanks to its lightweight componentry and those optional carbon wheels.

Pricing

The SV range starts at £139,995, with the SV Black at £154,975, and this ultimate Carbon edition at £165,045 (excluding further bespoke upgrades).
